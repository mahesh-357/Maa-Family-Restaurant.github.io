Our Story

About Maa Family Restaurant

brings the authentic taste of traditional Telugu home-style cooking to Hanamkonda. Inspired by age-old recipes passed down through generations, our kitchen focuses on rich flavors, fresh ingredients, and soulful meals that feel just like home.

We specialize in classic Telangana and Andhra dishes, prepared with care using traditional spices and cooking methods. Whether you're craving spicy Kodi Vepudu, comforting Pulusu, or wholesome Raagi Sankati, every dish reflects our love for Telugu cuisine.

Our warm ambiance, quick service, and family-friendly atmosphere make Alanati Telugu Kitchen a perfect choice for lunch, dinner, and casual dining with friends and family.

"Come hungry, leave happy — with memories of true Telugu flavors."
